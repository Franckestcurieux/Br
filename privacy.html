Privacy Policy

📦 Data Collection

This application does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind. No information about you or your usage is ever sent to any server.

💾 Local Storage Only

Exercise statistics (scores, progress, verb history) are stored exclusively on your own device using the browser's local storage mechanism. This data never leaves your device.

🔊 Text-to-Speech

The pronunciation feature uses your device's built-in speech synthesis system (Apple, Google or system TTS). No audio is recorded or transmitted.

🚫 No Account Required

The app requires no account, no login, no email address. There is no registration of any kind.

📡 No Tracking

There are no analytics, no advertising trackers, no cookies, no third-party scripts of any kind.

In summary: the app works entirely offline on your device. No personal data is collected — ever.
